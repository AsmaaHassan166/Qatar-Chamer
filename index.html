Qatar Chamber

Established in 1963 by virtue of Law No (4) of the year 1963, Qatar Chamber is considered one of the oldest chambers of commerce among GCC chambers.

It’s a non-profit public organization whose mission is to represent, support and protect the interests of the business community in Qatar.

Our services

Membership Renewal

Members (institutions/companies) registered with the Chamber should renew their membership in order to keep practicing their economic activities.

Attestation on Signature

Approving and attesting the authenticity of a signature on documents.

ATA Carnet
